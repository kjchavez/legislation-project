Welcome

Welcome. There is a wealth of data about the proceedings of Congress and it's all part of the public record. The U.S. Legislation Project is an effort to make this data more informative and actionable for the private citizen. We create and provide interactive access to machine learning models that explore many aspects of congressional activities.

Heads up! This is an ongoing personal project and may change rapidly and drastically without notice.

The Data

The primary data source is the bulk raw data from GovTrack https://www.govtrack.us/developers/data. The service for downloading the bulk data is now deprecated. The data sources, however, are still available at github:unitedstates/congress-legislators and github:unitedstates/congress.

We can't provide a full description of all the data provided by GovTrack, but some of the pieces that we use most heavily are:

  • Full text and revisions of proposed bills, resolutions, etc.
  • Roll call vote results
  • Congress member contact info and biographical data
